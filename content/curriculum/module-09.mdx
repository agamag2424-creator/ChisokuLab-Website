## What You'll Learn

Understand Sattva (clarity), Rajas (activity), and Tamas (inertia) in decision-making. Recognize which mode you're operating in.

## Key Takeaways

- Understanding the three gunas
- Identifying guna states in yourself
- Shifting from Tamas/Rajas to Sattva
- Practical applications in leadership

## Deliverable

**Guna Assessment Tool** - Framework to assess and shift your decision-making mode.
